Welcome to Gusage!

Gusage is a tool for photographers, designers and other visual artists which aims finding the usage in the web of your images and photographs, enabling you to:

  • Know where you work is being used
  • Check if users are meeting the standards for authorship defined in the publication of the image
  • See the impact of your work in internet: Press, social, shares and more!
